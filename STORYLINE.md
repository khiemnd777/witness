# The Witness - Storyline Roadmap

This document is the story roadmap before chapters are converted into runtime JSON, quest data, 3D scenes, or UI flows. Its purpose is to keep the overall narrative clear: the player is an ordinary witness who participates through small acts of service, listening, observation, and reflection.

## Story Principles

- The player does not alter the major biblical events; the player witnesses and serves at the edge of the story.
- Each chapter should have a central place, a slice of daily life, a sacred moment, and a reflection.
- Jesus is not a normal quest NPC, a controllable character, a trade target, or an ordinary gameplay objective.
- Progression should move from small acts toward larger meaning: care, listening, following, faith, loss, and hope.
- Ordinary characters should draw the player into the setting: pilgrims, shepherds, fishermen, sick people, gatekeepers, messengers, and secondary disciples.

## Chapter Structure

Each chapter should define:

- `Premise`: the biblical event and the player's viewpoint.
- `Player Role`: who the player is in ordinary life.
- `Quest Arc`: 3-4 short quests that lead toward the central moment.
- `Sacred Moment`: a reverent scene to witness, not a direct manipulation challenge.
- `Reflection Theme`: the spiritual theme at the end of the chapter.
- `Bible Anchor`: the scripture passage anchoring the content.

## Act I - Hope Arrives

### Chapter 01 - The Birth

- `Location`: Bethlehem
- `Status`: implemented as content
- `Premise`: A young local near Bethlehem helps travelers during the census and witnesses hope arriving in a humble place.
- `Player Role`: a local resident who knows the roads, the market, and possible places of shelter.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Ask the innkeeper about lodging.
  - Find clean supplies to help prepare the stable.
  - Hear the shepherds' message.
  - Observe the signs leading toward the manger.
- `Sacred Moment`: witness the infant Jesus in the manger with reverence.
- `Reflection Theme`: Humility, Hope, Worship
- `Bible Anchor`: Luke 2:1-20, Matthew 2:1-12

### Chapter 02 - In My Father's House

- `Location`: Jerusalem Temple
- `Status`: implemented as content; scene support still needs runtime work
- `Premise`: The player helps Passover pilgrims in Jerusalem, listens in the temple courts, and witnesses young Jesus among the teachers.
- `Player Role`: a helper near the temple gates.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Help tired pilgrims arriving at the temple gates.
  - Bring water and guide families toward the proper court.
  - Listen to the teachers in the temple court.
  - Stand with Mary after Jesus is found.
- `Sacred Moment`: Jesus speaks about His Father's house; the player witnesses from a reverent distance.
- `Reflection Theme`: Listening, Obedience, Wisdom
- `Bible Anchor`: Luke 2:41-52

### Chapter 03 - The Voice in the Wilderness

- `Location`: Jordan River Wilderness
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: Reports about John the Baptist draw crowds into the wilderness. The player helps groups find the river, hears the call to repentance, and witnesses the beginning of Jesus' public ministry.
- `Player Role`: a water carrier and guide for people gathering near the Jordan.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Guide a group toward the place where John is preaching.
  - Help a hesitant person understand the call to repentance.
  - Prepare the riverbank area for the crowd.
  - Observe Jesus' baptism.
- `Sacred Moment`: heaven opens, the Father's voice affirms the Son, and the Spirit descends; present this through light, sound, and crowd reaction rather than direct player action.
- `Reflection Theme`: Repentance, Identity, Calling
- `Bible Anchor`: Matthew 3:1-17, Mark 1:1-11, Luke 3:1-22

## Act II - The Kingdom Near Us

### Chapter 04 - Come and See

- `Location`: Galilee Shore and Capernaum
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: The player lives near the fishing boats and helps local fishermen and villagers as Jesus begins calling disciples.
- `Player Role`: a dockside helper familiar with fishing families.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Help mend nets and sort fish after a failed night.
  - Carry messages between families as crowds gather.
  - Walk with a fisherman toward the shore to hear Jesus.
  - Witness the first disciples leaving their nets to follow Him.
- `Sacred Moment`: the disciples leave their nets and follow Jesus.
- `Reflection Theme`: Calling, Trust, Surrender
- `Bible Anchor`: Matthew 4:18-22, Luke 5:1-11, John 1:35-51

### Chapter 05 - Water Into Joy

- `Location`: Cana Wedding
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: During a wedding feast in crisis, the player helps the servants keep order and witnesses the first sign.
- `Player Role`: a helper serving at the wedding.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Help welcome guests and locate missing supplies.
  - Hear the family's concern when the wine runs out.
  - Follow the servants' work by preparing the water jars.
  - Observe joy returning to the feast.
- `Sacred Moment`: water becomes wine, shown through the servants' and steward's reactions rather than spectacle.
- `Reflection Theme`: Joy, Obedience, Hidden Glory
- `Bible Anchor`: John 2:1-12

### Chapter 06 - Blessed Are

- `Location`: Hillside near Galilee
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: Crowds gather on a hillside. The player helps the elderly, children, and the poor find places to sit and hear Jesus teach about the kingdom.
- `Player Role`: one of the crowd, helping keep pathways clear and sharing simple supplies.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Lead a family safely up the hillside.
  - Help someone overlooked find a place near the teaching.
  - Listen to the main beats of the sermon.
  - Carry a teaching back to someone who needs encouragement.
- `Sacred Moment`: Jesus' teaching overturns expectations about blessing, power, mercy, and purity.
- `Reflection Theme`: Mercy, Meekness, Kingdom
- `Bible Anchor`: Matthew 5-7, Luke 6:20-49

### Chapter 07 - Rise and Walk

- `Location`: Capernaum House and Streets
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: A house is packed with people listening to Jesus. The player helps the friends of a paralyzed man find a way to bring him close.
- `Player Role`: a neighbor who knows the paths around the house.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Help clear a route through the crowd.
  - Find the sick man's family and friends.
  - Observe the friends' persistence from the rooftop.
  - Witness forgiveness and healing.
- `Sacred Moment`: Jesus forgives the man and tells him to rise.
- `Reflection Theme`: Faith, Forgiveness, Compassion
- `Bible Anchor`: Mark 2:1-12, Luke 5:17-26

## Act III - Seeing and Following

### Chapter 08 - Bread in the Wilderness

- `Location`: Remote Hillside
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: Crowds follow Jesus into a desolate place. The player helps organize families, care for children, and witness a meal provided for many.
- `Player Role`: a helper organizing the crowd.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Reunite family groups that have become separated.
  - Help the disciples arrange people into seated groups.
  - Carry news about the small offering of bread and fish.
  - Observe food being shared throughout the crowd.
- `Sacred Moment`: five loaves and two fish feed many; focus on compassion and sufficiency.
- `Reflection Theme`: Provision, Gratitude, Trust
- `Bible Anchor`: Matthew 14:13-21, Mark 6:30-44, John 6:1-15

### Chapter 09 - Who Is My Neighbor?

- `Location`: Road and Village near Jericho
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: The player hears the parable of the Good Samaritan, then faces an ordinary situation that requires mercy.
- `Player Role`: a traveler moving between the market road and an inn.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Hear the conversation about the greatest commandment.
  - Help an injured person by the road in a parallel situation without mechanically reenacting the parable.
  - Find oil, bandages, and a place to rest.
  - Reflect on the question, "Who is my neighbor?"
- `Sacred Moment`: Jesus' teaching shifts the focus from identity to merciful action.
- `Reflection Theme`: Mercy, Neighbor, Costly Love
- `Bible Anchor`: Luke 10:25-37

### Chapter 10 - On the Mountain

- `Location`: High Mountain and Galilee Foothills
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: The player travels with people waiting at the foot of the mountain and senses the gap between limited understanding and revealed glory.
- `Player Role`: a follower in the larger group, caring for those who wait below.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Help prepare a resting place below the mountain.
  - Listen to disciples discussing what they do not understand.
  - Support a family in need while the group waits.
  - Witness the disciples' changed response after they return.
- `Sacred Moment`: the Transfiguration is conveyed through distant light, sound, and the awe of indirect witnesses.
- `Reflection Theme`: Glory, Fear, Listening
- `Bible Anchor`: Matthew 17:1-13, Mark 9:2-13, Luke 9:28-36

## Act IV - The Road to Jerusalem

### Chapter 11 - The Road Up

- `Location`: Road to Jerusalem
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: Jesus and the group travel toward Jerusalem. The atmosphere shifts from public hope to tension, misunderstanding, and the invitation to take up the cross.
- `Player Role`: a pilgrim traveling with the larger group.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Help pilgrims prepare for the road up to Jerusalem.
  - Hear the disciples argue about status.
  - Serve someone left behind on the road.
  - Listen as Jesus teaches that the greatest is the servant.
- `Sacred Moment`: Jesus speaks more plainly about the path of suffering.
- `Reflection Theme`: Servanthood, Cost, Courage
- `Bible Anchor`: Mark 10:32-45, Luke 18:31-34

### Chapter 12 - Hosanna

- `Location`: Jerusalem Gate and Temple Courts
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: The player enters Jerusalem amid shouts of praise, then senses the difference between the crowd's expectations and Jesus' true mission.
- `Player Role`: a Passover pilgrim in the crowd.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Help prepare the road into the city with other pilgrims.
  - Carry palm branches and guide children away from the crush of the crowd.
  - Observe Jesus entering the city in humility.
  - Follow the crowd toward the temple courts as tension rises.
- `Sacred Moment`: Jesus enters Jerusalem on a young donkey.
- `Reflection Theme`: Peace, Kingship, Misunderstood Hope
- `Bible Anchor`: Matthew 21:1-17, Luke 19:28-48, John 12:12-19

### Chapter 13 - The Table and the Garden

- `Location`: Upper Room and Gethsemane
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: The night becomes heavy. The player serves at the edge of the Passover meal and later witnesses the darkness of the garden.
- `Player Role`: a helper preparing the meal space and following from a distance.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Prepare the upper room with water, lamps, and the table.
  - Hear words about service, covenant, and love.
  - Follow the group toward Gethsemane.
  - Help a fearful disciple, while being unable to stop what is coming.
- `Sacred Moment`: the Last Supper and prayer in the garden remain solemn, with minimal player interaction.
- `Reflection Theme`: Love, Watchfulness, Surrender
- `Bible Anchor`: Luke 22:7-46, John 13-17

### Chapter 14 - At a Distance

- `Location`: Jerusalem Courtyards and Golgotha
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: The player can only stand at a distance, hear scattered reports, help grieving people, and witness the cost of love.
- `Player Role`: a Jerusalem resident caught in the events of the night and the following morning.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Find a path through the courtyards as rumors spread.
  - Help someone in panic find a loved one.
  - Bring water or cloth to someone grieving at the roadside.
  - Stand at a distance near Golgotha in silence.
- `Sacred Moment`: the cross is presented through distance, sound, darkness, and witness reactions; it is not turned into a gameplay challenge.
- `Reflection Theme`: Sacrifice, Grief, Faithfulness
- `Bible Anchor`: Luke 22:47-23:56, John 18-19

## Act V - Hope Restored

### Chapter 15 - Why Do You Seek the Living?

- `Location`: Garden Tomb and Jerusalem Streets
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: Early after the Sabbath, the player helps the women prepare spices, then hears the impossible news: the tomb is empty.
- `Player Role`: a helper near the garden and city gate.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Help prepare spices before dawn.
  - Find a safe path toward the tomb.
  - Witness confusion before the empty tomb.
  - Carry the news back to those hiding in fear.
- `Sacred Moment`: the resurrection is received through announcement and witness response.
- `Reflection Theme`: Hope, Wonder, New Life
- `Bible Anchor`: Luke 24:1-12, John 20:1-18

### Chapter 16 - On the Road

- `Location`: Road to Emmaus
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: The player walks with two sorrowful disciples, hears Scripture opened along the road, and recognizes hope when bread is broken.
- `Player Role`: a traveler joining others on the road away from Jerusalem.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Help the two disciples prepare for the journey.
  - Listen as they describe their disappointment.
  - Gather the Scripture passages referenced in conversation.
  - Return to Jerusalem with good news.
- `Sacred Moment`: Jesus is recognized in the breaking of bread, carried by warm light, silence, and emotional transformation.
- `Reflection Theme`: Scripture, Recognition, Burning Hearts
- `Bible Anchor`: Luke 24:13-35

### Chapter 17 - Peace Be With You

- `Location`: Locked Room in Jerusalem
- `Status`: planned
- `Premise`: The player helps fearful disciples behind locked doors, then witnesses peace replacing fear.
- `Player Role`: a messenger and helper among the first community of disciples.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Find disciples who are hiding.
  - Help calm someone afraid of arrest.
  - Bring testimony from resurrection witnesses.
  - Witness peace being given to the community.
- `Sacred Moment`: Jesus appears among the disciples; the player does not interact with Him directly, but witnesses peace spreading through the room.
- `Reflection Theme`: Peace, Forgiveness, Mission
- `Bible Anchor`: John 20:19-29, Luke 24:36-49

### Chapter 18 - To the Ends

- `Location`: Mount of Olives and Jerusalem
- `Status`: planned finale
- `Premise`: The story closes with the commission. The player looks back on the journey from Bethlehem to Jerusalem and is invited to live as a witness.
- `Player Role`: a witness within the first community.
- `Quest Arc`:
  - Gather symbols or memories from witnesses encountered in earlier chapters.
  - Help prepare the community to wait in prayer.
  - Hear the commission.
  - Complete the final reflection on bearing witness in ordinary life.
- `Sacred Moment`: the Ascension and the promise of the Spirit, presented as an open ending leading toward Acts.
- `Reflection Theme`: Witness, Promise, Mission
- `Bible Anchor`: Luke 24:50-53, Acts 1:1-11

- Jairus' Daughter and the Sick Woman - Mark 5: desperation, quiet faith, and healing.

## Implementation Notes

- `public/content/chapters/index.json` should list only chapters that are ready to appear in the UI.
- Each planned chapter in this document should be converted into JSON across `chapters`, `quests`, `dialogues`, `npcs`, `items`, `scene_targets`, and `bible_refs`.
- When adding a chapter to runtime, register the matching scene in `src/game/scene/SceneRegistry.ts`.
- If a chapter does not yet have its own 3D scene, it should not be marked `available: true` in the product manifest.
